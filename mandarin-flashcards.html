About This Tool

All 150 words from HSK Level 1 — China's official beginner Chinese proficiency standard. HSK1 lets you handle greetings, numbers, time, daily objects, and simple sentences.

Flashcards: See the Chinese character. Tap to reveal pinyin + English meaning + example sentence. Rate yourself: Know / Fuzzy / Again. Your progress is saved in your browser.

Stories: Read an English story first. Then reveal the Chinese version. Tap any Chinese character to look it up instantly. Characters from HSK1 show full detail; others show basic info.

No login. No tracking. No cost. Data stays on your device.
